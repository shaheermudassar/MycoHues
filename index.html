Natural Origin

Fungal pigments are naturally occurring compounds, making them safer and more environmentally friendly than synthetic pigments.

Vibrant Colors

Fungal pigments produce a range of vibrant and unique colors, which can be difficult to replicate with synthetic pigments.

Cost-effective

Fungal pigments are more cost-effective than synthetic pigments, as they can be easily and sustainably produced using cultivation techniques.

Versatility

Fungal pigments can be used in a wide range of applications, including food and cosmetic colorants, and as a source of natural dyes.

Stability

Fungal pigments have shown to be more stable and more resistant towards the fading of color than that of synthetic pigments.

Our fungal pigments are undergoing rigorous testing procedures to ensure they meet the highest standards of safety and effectiveness.

Why Fungal Pigments?

Pigments, derived from fungi, provide a wide range of vibrant and natural color options for food and beverage products. Unlike synthetic food dyes, fungal pigments are made from all-natural sources, making them a safe and sustainable option for food coloring. Since they are formed by natural resources, they are cheaper compared to synthetic pigments
